Binen Gladys

"Binen Gladys shines not only with her intelligence, but with a heart full of compassion. She is the kind of person who makes everyone around her better, simply by being herself. Her presence is a gift, and her kindness a rare treasure."

Read more

Born on July 11th, 2000, Binen Gladys was Madeleine's eldest daughter and a beacon of responsibility, passion, and dedication. Deeply connected to her mother, she shared in every one of Madeleine's dreams and supported them with an unmatched devotion. Completing her studies at Mount Kenya University in 2022, she was not only an enthusiastic student but also a responsible woman who approached every endeavor with seriousness and care.

Married and known for her incomparable character, she was a cornerstone in the foundation of Madeleine Garden. Tragically, she passed away on February 11th, 2024, in the same accident as her beloved mother. Yet, her spirit of determination and love continues to inspire the legacy of Madeleine Garden. Every detail of this venue echoes her heart, as her devotion and character have left a lasting imprint.

Dieudonne Unencan

"Dieudonne Unencan embodies humility in its purest form. Despite his many successes, he always remains accessible and attentive, preferring to let his actions speak for him. His humility inspires respect and admiration, and his quiet leadership is an example for all who meet him."

Read more

Madeleine's husband, Dieudonne Unencan, is a man of great wisdom and intelligence, admired by all who know him. For over 20 years, he has been a dedicated mathematics teacher at the Lycée de Kigali (LDK), where his deep knowledge and thoughtful approach to teaching have left a lasting impact on his students. Married to Madeleine since 1999, he has always been a responsible and caring father, committed to his family and their well-being.

A man of calm philosophy and steady wisdom, he tragically lost both his beloved wife and daughter in a devastating accident. Despite this unimaginable loss, he remains a pillar of strength for his family. His ability to process the tragedy with grace and reflect deeply on life's challenges has earned him the respect and admiration of many.

As a loving father, he continues to guide and nurture his remaining children with the same care and thoughtfulness that have always defined him. Now, as the chief and responsible figure for Madeleine Garden, he plays a crucial role in continuing the legacy that Madeleine began. His leadership, wisdom, and dedication ensure that the values and vision Madeleine instilled in the garden live on.

His presence and contributions help preserve the spirit of Madeleine in every aspect of Madeleine Garden, making it not just a place of celebration, but also a tribute to the family's enduring strength, love, and unity.
